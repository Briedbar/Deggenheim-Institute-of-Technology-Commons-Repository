Access to scholarly works for all

Welcome to the Deggenheim Institute of Technology Commons Repository. This open-access resource offers a curated collection of scholarly works authored by DIT faculty and affiliated scholars. Our mission is to ensure free and equitable access to high-quality academic research across disciplines. Explore, engage, and contribute to a growing body of knowledge dedicated to public scholarship.
